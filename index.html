Cosmonaut Art Gallery

Cosmonaut presents itself to the city of Portland as the first fully open-door art gallery: Fully functional 24/7 with no appointments required.
We seek to bring printmaking and street art back to the people while promoting local and international artists.
Our community, and our city, are essential to our mission.
Come in, browse through our print archives or check out our latest exhibitions.
